The North East

Why North East businesses choose fractional CFO

The North East has a strong and growing ecosystem of ambitious businesses — from tech and professional services firms in Newcastle to manufacturing and logistics operations across Teesside and Sunderland. Many are reaching the scale where financial visibility, investor reporting, and commercial rigour are no longer optional.

A fractional CFO gives North East businesses access to the same quality of financial leadership that larger companies take for granted — without committing to a £120,000+ full-time salary. You get the strategic input when you need it, and the flexibility to scale up or down as your business evolves.

  • Revenue £1m–£40m and growing faster than your financial infrastructure
  • PE-backed or investor-funded, with reporting obligations you need to meet
  • Preparing for a funding round, acquisition or exit in the next 12–36 months
  • CFO or FD has left and you need experienced interim cover
  • The board or investors want more rigour from the finance function

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a fractional CFO cost in the North East?
Fractional CFO engagements typically run from £1,500–£4,000 per month depending on the number of days required. This compares to £10,000–£15,000 per month for a full-time CFO hire including employer costs.
Do you work on-site or remotely?
Both. Most engagements involve a mix of in-person time at your offices and remote working. We're based in Wynyard and work across the North East, Yorkshire and nationally.
How quickly can an engagement start?
Typically within one to two weeks of an initial conversation. We start with a scoping call to understand your situation before proposing an engagement structure.
